# 📊 Business Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Business Revenue Intelligence Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI**, with data sourced from **MySQL** and **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue performance, sales quantity, profit, market performance, customer insights, and year-over-year (YoY) growth.

It enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, analyze sales trends, identify top-performing markets and customers, and make data-driven business decisions through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

- Interactive Year Filter (2017–2020)
- Zone-wise Filtering

- Revenue Trend Analysis
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Revenue Comparison
- Sales by Market Analysis
- Revenue Distribution by Zone
- Top 5 Customers by Revenue

- Dynamic and Interactive Dashboard

## 📂 Dashboard Insights

### Revenue Analysis
- Monitor total business revenue.
- Analyze monthly revenue trends.
- Compare current revenue with previous year's performance.

### Sales Analysis
- Track total sales quantity across different markets.
- Identify high-performing regions and customer segments.

### Customer Insights
- Discover the Top 5 customers contributing the highest revenue.
- Understand customer purchasing patterns.

### Market Performance
- Compare revenue generated by different markets.
- Evaluate sales quantity and revenue contribution percentages.

### Zone Analysis
- Analyze revenue distribution across North, South, and Central zones.

### Year-over-Year Growth
- Measure YoY revenue growth to identify seasonal trends and business performance.

## ⚙️ How to Use

1. Clone the repository:

```bash
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/Business-Revenue-Intelligence-Dashboard.git
```

2. Open the `.pbix` file using **Microsoft Power BI Desktop**.

3. Connect to your MySQL database if required.

4. Refresh the dataset.

5. Explore the dashboard using:
   - Year Filter
   - Zone Filter
   - Interactive Charts
   - Market Performance Table
